Hotkeys map:

Left/Right keys
- Backward/Forward
Up/Down keys
- Volume up/down
M
- Toggle mute
T
- Toggle Theater Mode
Shift + P
- Play previous video on playlist
Shift + N
- Play next video on playlist
<
- Slow down the video
>
- Speed up the video
Home/End
- Beginning/End of video
0-9
- Set the time to (number + 0) % , e.g. 10%
F
- Full-screen
Double-click video
- Full-screen
